How to make AVOCADO TOAST!!!

Description

I make this tasty breakfast treat almost every morning. Obviously, everyone likes there avocado toast their own way, therefore there is a lot of flexibility within this recipe. However, after many mornings of trial and error, I have found that this is the perfect recipe for me, and I greatly encourage you to try it out for yourself!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Avocado
  • Cherry Tomatos
  • Balsamic Glaze
  • Honey
  • Everything but the Bagel Seasoning
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Toast your bread. (I usually just pop it into my toaster oven)
  2. Mash your avocado
  3. Spread mashed avocado onto toast
  4. Cut up cherry tomatos and place them on toast
  5. Drizzle honey and balsamic glaze onto toast
  6. Sprinkle on "Everything but the Bagel Seasoning" and salt and pepper
  7. Bon Appetite!
