About Me

Hi! My name is Gillian Schuh. I am 19 years old, and I am a sophomore at Parsons School of Design. I moved to New York City one year ago in the midst of the pandemic. I initially struggled to make the city my home as I lived in the dorms and struggled to make friends. Now, I live in Chelsea with my best friend Lydia and could not be happier with my decision to live here.
I am currently majoring in Strategic Design and Management with a minor in Communication Design. I love graphic design as well as studying art history and design studies. I currently am nannying a 6 year old boy named Silas. He loves to eat cucumbers and play roblox! And also play with pokemon cards...
